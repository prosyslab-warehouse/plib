Contacting Us - and Contributing to PLIB.

There are three PLIB mailing lists: In order to keep the spam off the lists, it's set up so you have to subscribe in order to send mail. To do that, visit:
  • http://lists.sf.net/mailman/listinfo/plib-announce
    A very low traffic, heavily moderated list where I'll announce new versions of PLIB - you may not post to 'plib-announce'.

  • http://lists.sf.net/mailman/listinfo/plib-users
    A list for PLIB users - this would be the correct place to ask questions, report bugs and generally hang out with fellow sufferers.

  • http://lists.sf.net/mailman/listinfo/plib-devel
    A list for people who wish to contribute to the development of PLIB - this list will have a high technical content and we'll try to keep discussions there focussed and on-topic.

Please do not send me personal mail in situations when one of the public mailing lists would be an appropriate forum - I hate it when people do that and I'll usually ignore you.

The mailing list is set up to prohibit large attachments. HTML mail and other non-ASCII forms of posting are NOT welcome.
